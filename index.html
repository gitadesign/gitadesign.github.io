About Me

Hey! I'm Gita, a Graphic Designer with 18 years of experience bringing brands to life across the globe. Based in Bromsgrove, I've worked as the lead designer for international companies like Air Liquide and Dinex, guiding creative projects from concept to delivery across multiple countries.

My journey has taken me from capturing product photography to optimising creative workflows, from designing pub menus to managing global asset libraries. I believe great design isn't just about what looks good - it's about what works, what communicates, and what makes people stop and pay attention.

When I'm not immersed in Adobe Creative Suite, you'll find me catching up on the latest movies or planning my next travel adventure, always seeking fresh inspiration along the way.
